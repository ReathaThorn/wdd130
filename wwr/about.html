About Us

Our History

Founded in 1995, White Water Rafting has guided thousands of adventurers through the most scenic and challenging rivers in the country. What started as a small family business with one raft has grown into a full adventure company with a reputation for safety, fun, and unforgettable memories.

Our Adventure

From peaceful scenic floats to high-adrenaline rapids, we offer experiences for every level of rafter. Our mission is to bring people closer to nature while ensuring every trip is safe, exciting, and memorable.
