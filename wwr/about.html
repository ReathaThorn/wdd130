About Battambang White Water Rafting

Our Mission

Our mission is to create safe, exciting, and unforgettable rafting experiences for adventurers of all ages. We aim to connect people with nature and the beautiful rivers of Cambodia.

Our Story

Founded in 2020, Battambang White Water Rafting started with a small group of passionate guides who wanted to share the joy of river adventures with others. Today, we are one of Cambodia’s most trusted rafting tour companies.
