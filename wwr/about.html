Our History

Since our founding, White Water Rafting has been dedicated to creating unforgettable outdoor adventures. What started as a small family-guided service has grown into one of the most trusted rafting outfitters, providing safe, fun, and thrilling river experiences for people of all ages.

Adventure Awaits You!

Whether you're looking for an adrenaline-pumping ride down Class V rapids or a peaceful float along scenic rivers, our adventures are designed to fit every level of excitement. Come join us and make lifelong memories with family and friends!
